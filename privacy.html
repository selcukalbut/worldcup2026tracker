Privacy Policy

WorldCup2026Tracker

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

WorldCup2026Tracker does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

Local Data

The app may store user preferences locally on the device, such as favorite teams, tracked matches, notification preferences, and app settings. This data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for match reminders, tracked matches, and tournament-related alerts. Notifications are generated locally on the device.

Third-Party Data

The app may retrieve publicly available football match data from third-party sports data providers. No personal information is shared with these providers.

Analytics

WorldCup2026Tracker does not use analytics tools that collect personal user information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the developer through the support information provided on the App Store listing.
